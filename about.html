About Me:

My name is Donovan Jones-Myers, I'm 23 years old, and I grew up in Minnesota. I'm both an engineer and an artist going to Morehouse for a degree in applied physics and computer engineering. Generally, I like to spend my time reading, yo-yoing, watching various things, listening to music, and doing art such as drawing, photography, playing music, or digital art.

When it comes to my work, I like to explore different things. Whether it's through the subject, lighting, medium, time of day, angles, colors, and so on, I try to find what speaks to me and pulls at my curiosity.
